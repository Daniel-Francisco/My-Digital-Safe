End-User Licence Agreement and Privacy Policy

This privacy policy sets out how “My Digital Safe” uses and protects any information that you give “My Digital Safe” when you use this app. “My Digital Safe” is committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. Should we ask you to provide certain information by which you can be identified when using this app, then you can be assured that it will only be used in accordance with this privacy statement. “My Digital Safe” may change this policy from time to time by updating this page. You should check this page from time to time to ensure that you are happy with any changes. This policy is effective from April 16th, 2018.

What we collect
We do not collect any of your data outside of what is locally stored on your device. None of your data will be transmitted away from your app, and we have no access to your data stored locally by "My Digital Safe".

What we do with the information we gather
We do not collect any of your information as we believe it is irresponsible to. "My Digital Safe" is dedicated to protecting your data and your privacy, and the collection of your private information would strictly go against our principles.

Security
We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. In order to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure we have followed industry standard methods of encryption to protect your password and data. We recommend you visit our Wiki on our GitHub Wiki page for more information. GitHub Wiki

Controlling your personal information
The protection of your encrypted personal information stored by "My Digital Safe" is ultimately up to you. We cannot control the protection of your Android device. Security vulnerabilities in the Android Operating System or the physical device can lead to the installation of malware outside of "My Digital Safe"'s control. Ultimately, if the phone is exploited by third party malware, there is little to nothing "My Digital Safe" can do to protect you. By agreeing to this Privacy Policy, you are agreeing to accept all responsibilities related to the protection of "My Digital Safe" data stored on the Android device.
