End-User Licence Agreement and Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how “My Digital Safe” uses and protects all information that you input to the app. “My Digital Safe” commits to protecting your privacy. The information you provide that identifies you as a user will be used in accordance with this privacy statement. “My Digital Safe” may change this policy from time to time by updating this page. You can check this page to ensure that you are up to date with any changes. This policy is effective from April 16th, 2018.

What we collect
The data that is collected and managed by "My Digital Safe" is locally stored on your Android device and will not be accessed, transmitted or stored outside of your device's local storage system.

What we do with the information we gather
"My Digital Safe" is dedicated to protecting your data and your privacy. Your private information is encrypted and stored with the intention of securely managing your data. "My Digital Safe" stores your information only for you to access it later. We will not manipulate, attempt to understand or track analytics on the information you store in "My Digital Safe".

Security
We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. In order to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure of your information, we follow industry standard methods of encryption to protect your password and data. For more information on our security practices, please visit our GitHub Wiki page at the link provided. GitHub Wiki

Controlling your personal information
"My Digital Safe" is limited to how it can ensure the protection of the data stored on your Android device. We cannot control the protection of your Android device as a whole. "My Digital Safe" cannot guarantee the protection of your encrypted data in cases where your Android device is compromised by malware among other third-party threats. By agreeing to this Privacy Policy, you accept responsibilities related to the protection of "My Digital Safe" data stored on your Android device.
